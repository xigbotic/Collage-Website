DMCA & Copyright

We don't host files. We just know where they are.

Content Disclaimer

Collage functions as a search engine and indexing tool. We do not host, upload, or store copyrighted audio files on our own servers. All files retrieved by commands (such as -leak or -repost) are hosted by third-party services or served directly from Discord's own CDN (Content Delivery Network).

We are a map, not a storage locker. If you have an issue with the content, take it up with the host.

Takedown Requests

If you are a copyright holder and believe a specific index entry infringes on your rights, you may contact us to have the metadata removed from our bot's search results.

  • We cannot delete the file from the internet (we don't control the host).
  • We can only stop our bot from pointing to it.

Contact commitarson@representative.com with the subject line "DMCA Request". Include proof of ownership. If you send us a fake request, we will ignore it and probably mock you.
