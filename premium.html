What Is Premium?

Premium is a service we offer that gives access to commands and exclusive features that regular users do not have access to.

DISCLAIMER: Premium is a service that is still in development, as is with the rest of the bot. These features may change or new features may be added at anytime.

Premium Commands & Features

  • -leaktracker - Sends every single known leak with dates to the user's DMs.
  • -date - Allows the user to specify a date, and the bot will find every leak from that date.
  • -comp - Provides an updated Mega Comp, consisting of over 4700 files!
  • -uzileak - Sends a specified song from Lil Uzi Vert.
  • -producer - Find all produced songs from a specified producer.
  • -convert - Convert any Youtube link into a MP3 file.
  • Early Access - Get invitations to early access builds of the bot and its features.
