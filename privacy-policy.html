Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: October 24, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Collage ("Bot"). This Privacy Policy explains how we ("We," "Our," or "Us") collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our Services on Discord. By adding our Bot to your server or using its commands, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

We only collect data that is necessary for the Bot to function properly.

A. Information You Provide Directly
  • Last.fm Username: When using -fmset, we store your username to connect to the Last.fm API. This is used solely for music features.
B. Automatic Data (Core Features)
  • IDs: User IDs, Server (Guild) IDs, and Channel IDs are stored to manage settings, economy, and prevent abuse (blacklists).
  • Command Usage: We process executed commands to provide responses. This is not stored long-term.
C. Special Data (Privileged Intents)
  • Snipe Command: Stores the content/author/timestamp of the most recently deleted message temporarily (RAM only). It is overwritten by new deletions or deleted automatically after ~10 minutes.
  • Leveling System: We process message content to award XP and prevent spam. We do not store message content for this feature. You can opt-out via -xpoptout.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • User Data (Last.fm, Levels): Stored until you request deletion.
  • Snipe Data: Temporary (RAM only), not stored in a persistent database.
  • Server Configs: Stored as long as the Bot remains in the server.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing. We only disclose information if required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of data stored about you.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of specific data (Last.fm, leveling progress).
  • Right to Opt-Out: Use -xpoptout to disable leveling tracking.

6. Age Restrictions

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, we will take immediate steps to remove it.

7. Security & Changes

Security: We implement industry-standard measures to prevent unauthorized access or alteration of your data.

Updates: We may update this policy. Significant changes will be posted here or announced in our support server.
