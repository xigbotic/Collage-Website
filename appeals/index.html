Ban Appeals

So, you messed up.

Before you start: We ban people for a reason. The chances of your appeal being accepted are statistically close to zero. But you are welcome to try.

Instructions

We do not use a web form because we don't want to store your excuses in our database. If you want to appeal, you must send an email.

  1. Ensure you have actually been banned and aren't just experiencing an internet outage.
  2. Write an email to commitarson@representative.com.
  3. Subject Line MUST be: [Ban Appeal] YourUsername#0000
  4. Include your Discord User ID (Turn on Developer Mode if you don't know how).
  5. Explain why you did it, and why we should care.
Draft Email

Automatic Rejections

Your appeal will be instantly deleted if:

  • You lie about what you did (we have logs).
  • You say "my brother used my account."
  • You spam our DMs asking to check your email.
  • You don't include your User ID.
